Meet Satyendra

With over fifteen years of styling experience across the globe's finest barbershops, Satyendra founded Eagle Men's Saloon to redefine modern men's grooming. He views barbering as an art form rather than a routine chore.

Known for his signature custom scissor cuts and unmatched straight-razor detailing, Satyendra caters to a clientele that appreciates precision, quality, and luxury. Booking a master session with Satyendra guarantees an individual style consultation, tailored recommendations, and an elite finish.
