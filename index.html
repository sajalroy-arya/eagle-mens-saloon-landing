Our Philosophy

A trade measured in millimetres

At Eagle, we do not believe in fast-paced assemblies or generic cuts. We understand that hair grows according to distinct patterns, and facial structure dictates the shape of a neckline or beard.

We work slowly, using hand-honed Japanese steel shears and traditional razors. Our salon is a place of calm, designed to allow you to disconnect while your barber focuses entirely on the details of your style.

"Barbering is not about trends. It is about geometry, growth patterns, and the patient application of skill."

— Satyendra, Founder
The Process

The Grooming Ritual

01

Structural Consultation

We discuss your hair density, crown directions, and face shape to determine a cut that sits naturally and grows out cleanly.

02

Precision Shear Cut

Clean scissor work and detailing utilizing Japanese carbon steel shears, tailored specifically to your hair texture.

03

Hot Lather Neck Shave

Warm eucalyptus lather is applied with badger-hair brushes, followed by straight-razor cleanups along the neck and ears.

04

Cold Compress & Finish

A cooling tea tree oil compress calms the skin, followed by a scalp massage and styling with organic styling clay.

Owner & Barber

Satyendra

Satyendra has spent over fifteen years studying head geometry, hair density patterns, and straight-razor detailing. He founded Eagle to return to the core fundamentals of classic barbering, stripping away modern shortcuts.

Specializing in scissor-only structuring and old-school hot lather shaves, Satyendra works deliberately to design styles that grow out gracefully. booking a Royal Session puts you directly in his chair for a customized consult and detailed grooming finish.
